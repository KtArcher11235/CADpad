## rationale
I needed a macropad to help with cad designing, eg, usefull and cimple keys that you dont even think about like ctrl and shift etc.
<br>
I used onshape and kicad for the designs, and decided on a cool rugged ish look.
<br>
## specifications
  BOM:  
1x Seeed XIAO RP2040  
6x Cherry MX Switches  
6x Blank DSA keycaps (White)  
6x SK6812 MINI-E LEDs  
4x M3x16mm screws  
optional (4x M3x5mx4mm heatset inserts)  
  OTHERS:  
firmware  
3d prints
